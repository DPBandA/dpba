Introduction

In metrology, measurement uncertainty is the expression of the statistical dispersion of the values attributed to a measured quantity. All measurements are subject to uncertainty and a measurement result is complete only when it is accompanied by a statement of the associated uncertainty, such as the standard deviation. This course aims to teach you the basics of measurement uncertainty and to get you started in determining, interpreting and applying measurement uncertainty statements.
